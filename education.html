Although my academic journey was interrupted by the outbreak of war, the knowledge and discipline I acquired during that time continue to influence my approach to learning and problem-solving.

This self-taught approach has allowed me to adapt to changing circumstances and stay motivated in pursuing personal and professional growth outside of traditional classroom settings.
